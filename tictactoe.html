Tic-Tac-Toe

Built with ClojureScript and Reagent during my apprenticeship at Clean Coders.

About this project

This was my final iteration of the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game, now playable in the browser! Features include multiple board sizes, player vs player, and player vs AI with multiple difficulty levels.
